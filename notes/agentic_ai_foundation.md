# Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF) - Latest News and Updates

## Overview

The **Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF)** was announced by the Linux Foundation on **December 9, 2025**, establishing a neutral, open foundation to ensure agentic AI evolves transparently and collaboratively. The foundation is anchored by three founding projects: **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** from Anthropic, **goose** from Block, and **AGENTS.md** from OpenAI.

The foundation's mission is to provide governance and support for open-source projects in the agentic AI space, with project inclusion based on demonstrated adoption, quality, and community health.

---

## Founding Projects

### Model Context Protocol (MCP)
- **Contributor:** Anthropic
- **Status:** Universal standard protocol with **over 10,000 published MCP servers**
- **Description:** MCP has rapidly become the industry standard for connecting AI systems with external tools and data sources

### goose
- **Contributor:** Block (parent company of Square, CashApp, and Tidal)
- **Description:** General-purpose AI agent for development and automation tasks

### AGENTS.md
- **Contributor:** OpenAI
- **Adoption:** Adopted by **over 60,000 open-source projects and tools** since its August 2025 release
- **Integrations:** GitHub Copilot, VS Code, Cursor, Gemini CLI, and many others

- **Date:** April 2-3, 2026

### Future Projects
Additional projects from member organizations have been proposed and are currently being evaluated for inclusion in AAIF. Selection criteria include:
- Demonstrated adoption in the market
- Strong governance structures
- Alignment with AAIF's mission of transparent and collaborative development

## Industry Context

According to **Gartner** predictions (December 2025):
- **40% of enterprise applications** will embed AI agents by the end of 2026
- This represents a dramatic increase from **less than 5%** in 2025
- Industry analysts are calling 2026 the "Year of Agentic AI"

This rapid growth underscores the importance of the Agentic AI Foundation's role in providing governance and standards for the emerging agentic AI ecosystem.
